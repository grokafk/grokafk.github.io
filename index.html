About Me

Hi, I'm Sean. Thanks for stopping by. I got into web design and development the long way. I have a Bachelor's in Landscape Design and a Master of Landscape Architecture. I have a deep understanding of design and it contributes immensely to my work every day. After the economy went to hell in 2009, I decided to head back to school and gain a skill set that was a little more future-proof. I've been in love with coding ever since, and front-end development gives me the opportunity to satisfy both my design brain and my code brain. Anyone who has worked with me will tell you I believe in taking ownership of a project, always closing the loop on communication, and am relentless in my pursuit of creative solutions. Give me a call, I'd love to hear what you've got going on.
